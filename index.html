Smart Revision Planner

The Smart Revision Planner is a web-based application that helps students prepare more effectively for exams by creating a structured and personalized study schedule. By simply entering their subjects and exam dates, students receive an automatically generated revision plan built on the principle of spaced repetition, ensuring better knowledge retention. The application also provides timely reminders to highlight the best moments for revision, helping students stay organized, consistent, and confident in their preparation.
